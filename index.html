About US

Jenny Home Care.

At Jenny Home Care, we are dedicated to providing compassionate and personalized home care services that promote independence, dignity, and well-being for seniors and individuals in need of assistance.
With a team of experienced, well-trained, and dedicated caregivers, we offer a wide range of services, including:

  • Personal care (bathing, dressing, grooming)
  • Companionship and emotional support
  • Medication reminders
  • Meal preparation
  • Light housekeeping and more

Whether your loved ones require short-term or long-term care, we are here to provide professional and warm-hearted support.

Our Services

We provide personalized care solutions designed to support everyday needs while ensuring comfort and well-being.

1.Personal care

Our personal care services focus on assisting with daily activities to maintain a clean, healthy, and comfortable lifestyle.

  • Dressing and Grooming.
  • Bathing; Baths, Showers, Bed baths.
  • Restroom assistance.
  • Nail care
  • Transition from Bed to Chair.
  • Oral and personal hygiene.
  • Walking, exercise assistance.
  • Taking Vital signs(RN/LPN/CNA).
  • Medication Reminders.

2. Companion care.

We understand the importance of companionship and emotional support for seniors.

Our caregivers provide assistance with:

  • Medical Pick ups.
  • Scheduling appointments.
  • Grocery shopping.
  • Light housekeeping.
  • Laundry services.
  • Meal preparation.

3. Hospital Visits.

We know that hospital visits can be stressful, whether for routine check-ups or extended stays. Our caregivers ensure that your loved ones are never alone during these important moments.

Our support includes:

  • Safe and reliable transportation to and from appointments.
  • Companionship during hospital stays or visits.
  • Assistance with paperwork, check-ins, and follow-ups
  • Communication with family members for updates.

4. Appointment Scheduling

Scheduling appointments can be challenging due to age, medical conditions, or language barriers.

At Jenny Home Care, we assist with:

  • Doctor’s visits
  • State, federal, and social agency appointments
  • Other essential scheduling needs
