Our Services

We offer a wide-range of personalized services tailored to fit your needs.

Personal care.

Personal care tasks means assistance with bathing, toileting, grooming, shaving, dental care, dressing, and eating; and may include but are not limited to proper nutrition, home management, housekeeping tasks, ambulation and transfer and medically related activities, including the taking of vital signs only in conjuction with the above tasks.
Below is a list of tasks:

  • Dressing and Grooming.
  • Bathing; Baths, Showers, Bed baths.
  • Restroom assistance.
  • Nail care
  • Transition from Bed to Chair.

  • Oral and personal hygiene.
  • Walking, exercise assistance.
  • Taking Vital signs(RN/LPN/CNA).
  • Medication Reminders.

Companion care.

Housekeeping or Housekeeping tasks means those activities performed for the upkeep and cleanliness of the home, including but not limited to such activities as laundry, changing linens, trash disposal and cleaning.
Below are some of companion care tasks:

  • Medical Pick ups.
  • Scheduling appointments.
  • Grocery shopping.
  • Light housekeeping.
  • Laundry.
  • Meal preparations.

Hospital Visits.

We know that hospital visits can be stressful, whether for a routine check-up or an extended stay. Our Hospital Visit Assistance service ensures that your loved ones are never alone during these important moments.

Our caregivers provide:

  • Safe and reliable transportation to and from appointments.
  • Support and companionship during hospital stays or visits.
  • Assistance with paperwork, check-ins, and follow-up instructions.
  • Communication with family members to provide updates.

Scheduling Appointments.

Scheduling appointments can be challenging to seniors due to physical and medical conditions, age, language barrier among other factor. At Jenny Home Care, we aid and assist with scheduling appointments with any agency; whether it's the doctor, state, federal or social agencies.
